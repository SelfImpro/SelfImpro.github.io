【翻译0】如果你想成功，你不得不放弃的13件事

发表于 2017-03-08   |     |   阅读次数

跳转原文

开始之前，也没有什么重要的启动或者宣誓，就是这样想学习英文，那就从这里开始，一周一篇译文，刚开始可以不限内容，嗯，剩下的就只是坚持。

Somebody once told me the definition of hell:
“On your last day on earth, the person you became will meet the person you could have become.” — Anonymous

有人曾经告诉我什么是地狱：
最后在这个地球上的日子，你成为的人将会遇到你可能成为的人 - 匿名

Sometimes, to become successful and get closer to the person we can become, we don’t need to add more things — we need to give up on some of them.
有时候，想要成功或者是想离自己想成为的人更进一步，我们不需要增加更多的东西，反而应该是放弃其中的一些。

There are certain things that are universal, which will make you successful if you give up on them, even though each one of us could have a different definition of success.
这里有一些具体的普遍性的事情，如果你远离他们将会是你成功。尽管每个人对成功的定义不尽相同。

You can give up on some of them today, while it might take a bit longer to give up on others.
尽管别人可能要花很久才能放弃，但是今天你必须放弃它们。

放弃不健康的生活方式

Give Up On The Unhealthy Lifestyle

Take care of your body. It’s the only place you have to live.
照顾好你的身体。因为这是活下去不可缺少的 - Jim Rohn

If you want to achieve anything in life, everything starts here. First you have to take care of your health, and there are only two things you need to keep in mind:
所有你想实现的都是从这里开始。首先你得关注自己的健康，这里只有两件事情你需要牢记:

  1. Healthy Diet 健康饮食
  2. Physical Activity 日常运动

Small steps, but you will thank yourself one day.
将来有一天你会感谢自己的这一小步。

放弃短期心态

Give Up The Short-term Mindset

“You only live once, but if you do it right, once is enough.” — Mae West
你只能活一次，但是能做对的事情，活一次也够了。

Successful people set long-term goals, and they know that these aims are merely the result of short-term habits that they need to do every day.
成功的人设定长期目标，他们知道这些目标只不过都是平时每天需要做的短期目标的综合结果。

These healthy habits should not be something you do; they should be something you are.
这些健康的习惯不应该是你做的事情；而应该是你的东西。

There is a difference between: “Working out to have summer body” and “Working out because that is who you are.”
就像“为了summer bodyh而工作”和“这个工作是我的使命”是有区别的。

放弃一些小事情

Give Up Playing Small

“Your playing small does not serve the world. There is nothing enlightened about shrinking so that other people will not feel insecure around you. We are all meant to shine, as children do. It is not just in some of us; it is in everyone, and as we let our light shine, we unconsciously give others permission to do the same. As we are liberated from our fear, our presence automatically liberates others.” - Marianne Williamson
你的一些小事情没有服务这个世界。没有什么事情能够启发退缩，所以其他人也不会在你这里感觉到安全感。我们都被告知像小孩子一样阳光。

If you never try and take great opportunities, or allow your dreams to become realities, you will never realize your true potential.
如果你从来没有尝试去抓住一个好机会，或者让自己的梦想成为现实，你将永远无法释放自己的潜能

Moreover, the world will never benefit from what you could have achieved.
此外，这个世界也不会从你取得的成就里获益。

So voice your ideas, don’t be afraid to fail, and certainly don’t be afraid to succeed.
所以讲出你的想法，不要害怕失败，当然也不用害怕成功，哈哈。

放弃你的借口

Give Up Your Excuses

“It is not about the cards you are dealt, but how you play the hand.” - Randy Pausch, The Last Lecture

Successful people know that they are responsible for their life, no matter their starting point, weaknesses, and past failures.
成功的人懂得对自己的生命负责，无论从哪里来，软弱，以及过去的失败。

Realizing that you are entirely responsible for what happens next in your life, is both frightening and exciting.
慢慢的去明白对自己生命即将发生的复杂，是一件令人恐惧，但同时也很令人兴奋。

However, it is the only way that you can reach the success because excuses limit and prevent us from growing personally and professionally.
当你这样做的时候，那将成为你成功唯一的路，因为借口限制和阻止了我们成长为独当一面以及专业的人。

Own your life; no one else will.
掌握自己的命运，舍我其谁。

放弃固化的思想

Give Up The Fixed Mindset

“The future belongs to those who learn more skills and combine them in creative ways.” ― Robert Greene, Mastery
未来属于那些学习更多技能，并将这些应用到创造性生活中的人。

In a fixed mindset, people believe that their intelligence or talent, are simply fixed traits and that talent alone creates success — without effort. They are wrong.
思维固化的人认为智力或者天赋是他们固有的特性，而且仅这种天赋就让可以使他们不用努力就能获得成功。他们都错了。

Moreover, successful people know this. They invest an immense amount of time on a daily basis to develop a growth mindset, acquire new knowledge, learn new skills and change their perception so that it can benefit their lives.
成功的人都知道。他们投资巨大的时间在日常基础上以获得一个思维上的成长

Remember, who you are today, it is not whom you have to be tomorrow.
记住今天你是谁，而不是明天你要成为的人。

放弃相信“魔法子弹”

Give Up Believing In The “Magic Bullet.”

“Every day, in every way, I’m getting better and better” — Émile Couém
每一天，每一条路，我都会努力让自己变得越来越好。

Overnight success is a myth.
突然的成功是一个神话

Successful people know that making small continual improvement every day will be compounded over time, and give them desirable results.
成功的人都知道每天持续的提高随着时间会慢慢的积累，会带给他们满意的结果。

That is why you should plan for the future, but focus on the day that’s ahead of you, and improve just 1% every day.
这也是为什么你需要一个长远的计划，但是关注当下是你当前最重要的，同时每天提高百分之一。

放弃你的完美论

Give Up Your Perfectionism

“Shipping beats perfection.” — Khan Academy’s Development Mantra
Nothing will ever be perfect, no matter how much we try.
无论你多么努力，没有什么是完美的。

Fear of failure (or even fear of success) often prevents us from taking an action and putting our creation out there in the world. But a lot of opportunities will be lost if we wait for the things to be right.
害怕失败（或者也害怕成功）经常阻碍我们的行动，阻碍我们在这个世界发挥我们的创造力。

So “ship,” and then improve (that 1%).
所以 ship ，然后提高自己（那个百分之一）。

放弃多任务

Give Up Multi-tasking

“You will never reach your destination if you stop and throw stones at every dog that barks.” ― Winston S. Churchill
如果你停止并且向每一个对你咆哮的狗扔石头，你将永远也无法到达你的目的地。

Successful people know this. That’s why they choose one thing and then beat it into submission. No matter what it is — a business idea, a conversation, or a workout.
成功的人都知道。那就是为什么他们选择一件事情，然后直到完成它。无论这件事情是什么——一个商业想法，一次对话，或者一个实验

Being fully present and committed to one task, is indispensable.
现在全身心并坚定的投入一个任务中，是不可少的。

放弃你需要的去控制的一切

Give Up Your Need to Control Everything

“Some things are up to us, and some things are not up to us.” — Epictetus, Stoic philosopher
有些事情取决于我们，有些事情不属于我们。

Differentiating these two is important.
认清这两件事的不同是很重要的。

Detach from the things you cannot control, and focus on the ones you can, and know that sometimes, the only thing you will be able to control is your attitude towards something.
脱离那些你无法控制的事情，聚焦在你能做的事情上，有时候你就会意识到，一件你可以掌控的事情往往取决于你的态度。

Remember, nobody can be frustrated while saying “Bubbles” in an angry voice.
记住，没有人能够做到用生气的语气讲 Bubbles 时表现得很颓废。

放弃对不能支持你目标的事情

Give Up On Saying YES To Things That Don’t Support Your Goals

“He who would accomplish little must sacrifice little; he who would achieve much must sacrifice much; he who would attain highly must sacrifice greatly.” — James Allen
一个人如果得到的少，那么他肯定付出的也少；如果得到的很多，那么他付出的也很多；如果想得到更多，就必须付出更多。

Successful people know that in order to accomplish their goals, they will have to say NO to certain tasks, activities, and demands from their friends, family, and colleagues.
成功的人都知道，如果想实现他们的目标，他们就必须对一些来自他朋友，家庭，同行的任务，活动，需求说No。

In the short-term, you might sacrifice a bit of instant gratification, but when your goals come to fruition, it will all be worth it.
在短期任务中，你可能牺牲了很多短暂的快了，但是当你的目标开始实现了，一切将会是值得的。

远离有毒的人

Give Up The Toxic People

“You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with.”
― Jim Rohn
你就是你身边花时间最多的五个人的平均水平（指各方面能力）。

People we spend the most time with, add up to who we become.
我们花时间跟谁在一起，决定了你将要成为什么样的人。

There are people who are less accomplished in their personal and professional life, and there are people who are more accomplished than us. If you spend time with those who are behind you, your average will go down, and with it, your success.
有些人在个人生活和职业生涯中获得很少，有些人获得的远比他们多。如果你花时间那些落后你的人，你的均值也会降低，如果是后者，你就好成功。

But if you spend time with people who are more accomplished than you, no matter how challenging that might be, you will become more successful.
但是如果你花时间跟比你付出还多的人，无论那是什么挑战，你会更成功。

Take a look at around you, and see if you need to make any changes.
看看四周，看看是否有什么需要改变。

放弃你的那点需要被喜欢的情结

Give Up Your Need To Be Liked

“The only way to avoid pissing people off is to do nothing important.” — Oliver Emberton
避免惹怒别人的唯一方式就是不做任何重要的事情。

Think of yourself as a market niche.
假设自己是一个利基市场

There will be a lot of people who like that niche, and there will be individuals who don’t. And no matter what you do, you won’t be able to make the entire market like you.
无论你做什么，将会很多喜欢这个市场的人，也有很多不喜欢的个，你无法让整个市场都喜欢你。

This is entirely natural, and there’s no need to justify yourself.
这些完全是自然现象，完全没有必要去证明自己。

The only thing you can do is to remain authentic, improve and provide value every day, and know that the growing number of “haters” means that you are doing important things.
唯一你能做的就是保持真实，每天提高并提供价值，要记住如果有越来越多的反对你的人，说明你在做一件重要的事情。

放弃依赖社交媒体和电视

Give Up Your Dependency on Social Media & Television

“The trouble is, you think you have time” — Jack Kornfield
你想你有时间，这是个问题！

Impulsive web browsing and television watching are diseases of today’s society.
These two should never be an escape from your life or your goals.
冲动的网页浏览和看电视在今天这个社会是一种病。这两者从未从你的生活或者目标中离开。

Unless your goals depend on either, you should minimise (or even eliminate) your dependency on them, and direct that time towards things that can enrich your life.
除非你的目标建立在两者之上，否则你需要尽可能减小这种依赖，将时间用在能够直接让你生活更加丰富的事情上。
